PRISM LEARNING CENTER - Your Premier Partner for Excellence in the Philippine Architecture Licensure Examination (ALE) and Creative Arts Development. Located at Wilcon City Center Visayas Avenue - Second Level. 

It was founded by Architect Josefina Bautista-Alfonso in 2024 to provide a better place  for  Architecture Review preparation. It offers comprehensive review programs for future architects, provides review materials, and offers online/onsite review coaching, art and leadership workshops, and masterclasses.

Architecture Licensure Examination (ALE) Review Program

PRISM Learning Center offers a meticulously designed review program tailored specifically to the needs of aspiring architects preparing for the Philippine Architecture Licensure Examination. 

Our program is led by highly qualified mentors from distinguished institutions, including topnotchers and experienced professionals, ensuring you receive expert guidance and insights. With a structured curriculum covering all essential aspects of the ALE, we are committed to equipping you with the knowledge and skills necessary for success in the licensure exam.
