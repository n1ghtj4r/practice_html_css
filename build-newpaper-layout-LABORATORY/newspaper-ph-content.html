Bagong Pilipinas: Marcos Jr. Unveils Ambitious 10-Year Transformation Plan

President Ferdinand "Bongbong" Marcos Jr. yesterday launched the "New Philippines" (Bagong Pilipinas) initiative, a comprehensive roadmap aimed at transforming the country into a high-income economy by 2035. The plan focuses on massive infrastructure development, digital transformation, green energy, and education reform. "This is not just a vision — this is our commitment to the next generation," the President said during the launch at the Malacañang Palace.

PH Economy Grows 6.8% in Q2 2026

The Philippine economy posted a robust 6.8% growth in the second quarter, driven by strong BPO, tourism, and semiconductor exports. Economists credit the New Philippines reforms for the surge in foreign investments.

Subway System in Metro Manila to Open 3 New Lines by 2028

The Department of Transportation announced that three additional subway lines under the New Philippines infrastructure program will begin operations by 2028, easing traffic congestion in the capital.

Tourism Rebounds: Record 8.2 Million Visitors in First Half of 2026

Boracay, Palawan, and Cebu saw record arrivals as the country continues its "Visit the New Philippines" campaign. Tourism Secretary expects to break the 2019 record by year-end.

Digital Philippines: Free Wi-Fi and Tech Hubs Coming to Every Province

The government’s Digital Transformation Act under the New Philippines agenda promises free high-speed internet in all barangays and the establishment of 100 innovation hubs nationwide. DICT Secretary emphasized that this will empower MSMEs and bring opportunities to rural areas.
